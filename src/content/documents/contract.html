2. Scope of Work

3. Payment Terms

A deposit of 50% of the agreed project total is due before work begins. The remaining balance is due upon project completion and before final delivery of files or site launch, whichever comes first. Invoices unpaid after 14 days may incur a 1.5% monthly late fee.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 7 days' written notice. Upon termination, Company will invoice for all work completed to date. Deposits are non-refundable unless Company fails to begin work within an agreed timeframe.
